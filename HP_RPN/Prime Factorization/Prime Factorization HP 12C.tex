\section{How to use the Prime Factorization program}

\bigskip
Enter number N and run with \RStwc. The program will show all the prime factors of N from a low prime (2) and then up.

\bigskip
\noindent
Example:

\tt
\indent \NBRtwc{10} \\
\indent \RStwc \\
\rm

\bigskip

Output:\\
\it running \rm \\
\tt \NBRtwc {2.00} \indent \it (pause one second)\\
\it running \rm \\
\tt \NBRtwc {5.00} \\
\rm
